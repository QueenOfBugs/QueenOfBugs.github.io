1 虚拟机运行Guix

1.1 安装好后运行Guix

command: 

qemu-system-x86_64 \
                                      -nic user,model=virtio-net-pci \
                                      -enable-kvm -m 2048 \
                                      -device virtio-blk,drive=myhd \
                                      -drive if=none,file=$HOME/guix-system.img,id=myhd
